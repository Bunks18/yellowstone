Our Services


Tailored Travels offers complete travel planning services that are personalized based on your tastes, interests and budget. Whether your idea of adventure skews more toward rugged and daring or relaxed and pampering, Tailored Travels is for you. Share with us your spark of an idea on places you have longed dreamed of visiting, and we will take it from there.

We work with with:
  • Solo Travelers
  • Familes
  • Couples
  • Youth and Student Groups
  • Fly Fishing Anglers
  • Schools and Universities

How It Works


We start the process by asking these basic questions
Where do you want to go?
When do you want to go?
What is your level of fitness?
How many people?
What is your budget?
What are your top three goals?
